Steps to Prepare for the Trip

  1. Save money for travel expenses.
  2. Apply for a passport (and visa if needed).
  3. Book flights and accommodations.
  4. Learn basic Japanese phrases.
  5. Pack essentials and cultural gifts.

Travel Glossary

Shinkansen
The Japanese bullet train, known for speed and efficiency.
Onsen
A traditional Japanese hot spring bath.
Ryokan
A traditional Japanese inn with tatami floors and futon bedding.
